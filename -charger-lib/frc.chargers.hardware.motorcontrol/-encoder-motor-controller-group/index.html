EncoderMotorControllerGroup

open class EncoderMotorControllerGroup<C : HardwareConfiguration> : NonConfigurableEncoderMotorControllerGroup, HardwareConfigurable<C>

Similar to NonConfigurableEncoderMotorControllerGroup, a MotorControllerGroup that aggregates the encoders of its MotorControllers in addition to their speeds. However, it also allows for group configuration of all its MotorControllers at once.
